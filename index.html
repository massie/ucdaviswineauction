"Winter's Ball"


We cordially invite you to join us for an exciting evening of unique silent and live auction items, live music, friendly games, delectable food, and delicious wine. This year's theme is ‘Winter's Ball,' a "Blue" tie event. All proceeds go towards supporting nine of the UC Davis-Affiliated Student-Run clinics for the underserved populations of Sacramento.

The Silent Auction and Wine Tasting Benefit is a themed gala and the largest student-driven effort to raise money for the student run clinics at UCDSOM hosted by the MS2s with support from MS1s and various undergraduate volunteers at the clinics.

About Our Clinics

Who are we and whom do we benefit?


We are the largest Student Run fundraiser benefiting the student-run clinics affiliated with the UC Davis School of Medicine. The clinics provide free and comprehensive health care to the underserved patient populations of the Sacramento area.
